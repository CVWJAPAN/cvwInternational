Our Vision

Our mission is to build a society where no one is left behind — a world free from child abuse. We aim to achieve this by transforming welfare through digital innovation (DX), combining empathy, technology, and global solidarity.

Founder’s Story

My name is NOBUSHI. I was abandoned at 3 months old and raised in institutions where I experienced abuse. I have lived through 44 years of pain, isolation, and despair — even considering ending my life.

But I chose to live. Because I believe that children deserve joy, dignity, and safety. And someone must act. I will be that someone.

What is CVW?

CVW stands for Children’s Voice World. It is a global solidarity movement to amplify the voices of children who cannot speak out. Our goal is to create a decentralized support system that protects the most vulnerable.

Global and Japan Child Abuse Statistics

Every year, over 1 billion children worldwide experience physical, sexual, or emotional violence. Approximately 40,150 children lose their lives due to abuse or neglect. More than 300 million children are victims of online sexual exploitation annually.

In Japan, a record-breaking 225,509 child abuse cases were handled by child consultation centers in 2023:

  • Psychological abuse: 134,948 cases (59.8%)
  • Physical abuse: 51,623 cases (22.9%)
  • Neglect: 36,465 cases (16.2%)
  • Sexual abuse: 2,473 cases (1.1%)

In addition, 527 children died by suicide in 2024 — the highest number on record.

These are not just numbers. They are a call to action. Together, we can end this.
