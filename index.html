Our Mission

CVW INTERNATIONAL is a nonprofit born from the lived experience of institutional abuse and isolation. We believe in building technology that cares—AI that listens, understands, and protects. Our mission is to empower children who have no voice, no protection, and no support through globally scalable, ethical, and empathetic technology.

Projects

CVWCORE: Our secure AI platform providing support to children, caregivers, and communities.

CVW-REACT: An emotionally responsive AI engine built to detect not only risk, but resilience.

Digital Children's Centers: Safe, high-tech spaces where children connect with AI and human support.

Message from NOBUSHI

Hello, my name is NOBUSHI.

For 44 years, I have lived through pain and loneliness.
I have cried out for help that never reached anyone.
I have even thought about ending my life.

And yet, I am still here—because I believe I have a mission.
A mission to create a world where every child can smile and see a bright future.

Today, the world stands at a turning point.
It is time for a new beginning, and a new kind of international organization—
one that is born from truth, from survival, and from a deep love for children.

Please lend your strength to this movement.
CVW stands for Children's Voice World—
a vision to carry the voices of children across the globe.

Help us build a world without abuse.
Let us create a future no child has to survive, but can truly live.
